ABOUT SANCORN

Tradition Since 2020

The name "Sancorn" is a combination of the word popcorn and the owner Ihsan's nickname for "san" (Ih"san" + pop"corn"= sancorn). Ihsan has set out the product to be produced in 2020, began in kajang where he lived at the time. Teaming up with a factory suppliers, Ihsan made sure the product produce are of the highest quality to guarantee the finest taste of popcorn. Flavours of the popcorn are always changing consistently with the seasons to keep up with the new trends. We welcomes everyone to our service, especially teens and kids. According to a survey, kids and teens are the top lover of sweets. not only do we have sancorn, we also sell the most loveable dessert of all time, a chocojar.




By making great snacks, Sancorn helps keep families together, keeps friendships strong, and lets people enjoy the simple things in life. We want to be a global Malaysia brand that helps build the country, connects it to its neighbors, and cares about people and the environment.

•To provide clients with high-quality Sancorn products.
•With passion and dedication, deliver professional consultation and services to customers.
•To maximize profit and ensure that our shareholders receive the best possible returns on their investments.
